Kingscomo Security Guard Manages- its own security services on a territorial level using operators and supervisors employed by its employees.
To ensure excellent service, the supervisors of the Kingscomo Security Guard are:
• Free from criminal proceedings or pending charges.
• Provided with the necessary uniform with company logo, identification card and company mean of communication.
• Trained in firefighting and first aid.
• In possession of an insurance policy with a leading insurance company to cover any damage to property or people during the performance of their services.
• Regularly insured to social security institutions and paid according to the provisions of the national employment contract to which they belong.
• Trained in legal notions on safety activities, lawful conduct provided for by legislation and regulations on safety at work.
• With an impeccable civil and moral conduct, a sense of duty and human solidarity.
• They ensure maximum availability, confidentiality, reliability, firmness and decision, pleasant appearance, good education, patient and determined attitude.
